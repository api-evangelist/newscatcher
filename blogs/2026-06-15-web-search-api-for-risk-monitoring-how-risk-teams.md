Enterprise risk management software manages known internal risks, but a web search API adds the missing external layer to detect early signals across suppliers, cyber incidents, regulations, geopolitics, reputation, and market instability before they escalate. Using Amneal Pharmaceuticals as an example, the article outlines a six-step workflow (define risk queries, ingest signals continuously, qualify via deduplication and entity matching, score by severity, route to systems, and refine) and evaluation criteria including coverage depth, recall accuracy, freshness, noise reduction, structured outputs, and security controls.
