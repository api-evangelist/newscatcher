A tutorial on building an automated local business opening tracker using CatchAll's Web Search API. It covers discovering opening signals with targeted searches for phrases like "grand opening," "now open," and "soft opening," then validating results, extracting structured data, deduplicating findings, and routing them to outputs such as terminals, email alerts, and monitoring workflows.
